My Story

Cakes Made with Love

Hi, I’m Sandra, and my journey into custom cake making started with my biggest inspiration—my amazing son. He's the heart of everything I create, and all my work is dedicated to him. I find immense joy in crafting edible art that helps create cherished memories for others. Sharing my creativity and putting a unique touch on each cake has become my passion.

To ensure everyone can enjoy my cakes, I offer options for gluten-free, dairy-free, and vegan cakes. These special requests challenge me to push my creativity and continually improve. My ultimate goal is to make every experience with my cakes memorable and enjoyable for all.

Cake Cups

Cake cups, dessert cups, and dessert shooters—these delightful treats come in many forms. They consist of mini cups filled with layers of cake and delectable fillings that echo my signature cake flavors. Perfectly paired with a tiered cake, they add a charming touch to any dessert display. Two sizes are available: the 3oz option is ideal for smaller appetites or as an addition to another dessert, while the 5oz cup is a generous half size larger than a cupcake.

Custom cakes begin at $150, with additional elements like cake toppers, specialty fondant designs, florals, and substitutions priced separately.
Pricing for 6" and 8" two-tier cakes starts at $185.
